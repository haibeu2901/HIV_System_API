About ARV Medications

What is ARV?

Antiretroviral therapy (ART) uses HIV medicines to fight HIV infection, helping people live longer, healthier lives.

How it Works

ARV medications block different stages of the HIV life cycle, preventing the virus from replicating.

Benefits

Reduces viral load, strengthens immune system, and prevents transmission when taken consistently.
